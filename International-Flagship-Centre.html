What Is Param Experience Centre?

A dream that is fuelled by passion to bring History, Science and Culture to the people in one spectacular destination. A multidisciplinary space that promises to satiate curiosity and creativity while connecting us to our roots! A never before experiential hub that will draw people to our centre from all over the world. A unique Not for profit project powered by passionate individuals and reputed organisations. The aim is to showcase India's diverse culture and heritage along with her rich history and brilliant scientific minds in a uniquely immersive world created to amaze all visitors.

Science Zone

The first of its kind experiential science centre that will inspire and invigorate one and all. Get ready to be transported into the spectacular Paramverse guaranteed to make you fall in love with science.

History Gallery

Go back in time and experience history come alive through immersive and innovative story telling. Dive deep into the vibrant and diverse history of India that showcases the valour and resilience of one of the oldest civilisations in the world.

Incubation Center

Our incubation centre is an ideal space to bring your innovative ideas to life. if you believe your idea is transformative, let's work together to make it a reality.

Guest Rooms

Combined with our 250 room luxurious guest rooms, it is an ideal place for well rounded corporate conventions.
