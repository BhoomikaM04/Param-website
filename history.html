Param History Vertical At Param History Centre, our mission is to bring India's past to life through immersive, interactive storytelling that connects every visitor -young and old-with the nation's enduring legacy. We strive to foster a deep appreciation for our shared heritage by making history tangible, relatable, and inspiring.

Our Teams

Historians & Research Specialists

Curate authentic narratives by sourcing primary documents, oral histories, and archaeological insights.

Validate all content to ensure historical accuracy and diverse perspectives.

Exhibit Designers & Storytellers

Craft multisensory installations, dioramas, and narrative pathways.

Integrate AR/VR and interactive media for compelling visitor journeys.

Content Writers & Interpreters

Develop scripts, informational panels, and multimedia guid in multiple languages.

Collaborate with academic partners to translate complex history into accessible formats.

Technical & AV Team

Build projection-mapped reconstructions, holographic displays, and audio-visual experiences.

Maintain immersive theatre spaces and digital kiosks.

Community Outreach & Education Coordinators

Coordinate school programs, living-history workshops, and volunteer docent training.

Facilitate heritage walks, seminars, and public lectures in partnership with local communities.
