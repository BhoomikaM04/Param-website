About Us

Param Innovation Lab is building the next great innovation civilisation open, fearless, and powered by people. We exist to unlock the creative potential of any citizen by blending science that awakens curiosity, history that builds conviction, and culture that fuels bold imagination.
Our ecosystem spans public maker spaces, Innovation Labs, and dynamic Centres of Excellence, giving individuals the freedom and capability to explore, experiment, and turn ideas into groundbreaking solutions.
This is where minds activate, ideas accelerate, and the future takes shape - built by the community, for the community

Innovation Labs Philosophy

A Matrix Of Ideas, People, And Purpose

Param's teams don't operate in silos. Instead, we follow a matrix structure that enables rich cross-talk, shared learning, and integrated thinking. We call it our internal ecosystem.

Operate Like Bell Labs

Long-term vision, short fast cycles, and freedom to explore without bureaucratic drag.

Flat Structure

No rigid hierarchy; innovators, curators, and partners work as peers.

Project-Based Innovation

Contributors self-assign based on expertise and interest, dynamic team formation.

First-Principles Thinking

Solve from fundamentals, challenge assumptions, avoid "it's always done this way."

No Jugaads

Clever but fragile fixes aren't allowed; quality and reliability matter.

Fail Fast, Learn Faster

Rapid prototyping, early testing, iterate or discard with clear lessons.

Interdisciplinary Collaboration

Curators, engineers, designers, and industry professionals at the same table.

Open by Default

Use open-source tools, share knowledge unless there's a strategic/IP reason not to.
