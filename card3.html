Learning Like a Machine

English

06 to 10

Apr 12

11.00 AM

ParSEC Maker's Adda, jayanagar

About the Event

Participants step into the role of a "learning machine" to understand how Al learns from examples. Through physical games, sorting challenges, and group decision-making, they experience how patterns are identified, mistakes are made, and learning improves through feedback.
Set in the context of arriving on a new planet, participants begin working with their Al partner to make sense of unfamiliar resources-starting with identifying what might be useful for survival.
