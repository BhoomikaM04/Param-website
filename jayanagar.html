Parsec: Where Science Comes Alive

Welcome to Bengaluru's premier destination for "Edutainment"-where education meets entertainment. Located just 300m from the Jayanagar Metro, ParSEC is not just a museum; it is a spark for curiosity.
What Awaits You:

  • Immersive Galleries: Explore five distinct themed galleries designed to make science interactive and fun.
  • Personalized Tours: The best part of ParSEC? Our expert facilitators. Enjoy a personalized guided tour that brings every exhibit to life.
  • Live Science Shows: Witness the magic of physics and chemistry in our regular live demonstrations.

Maker's Adda: Build Beyond Theory Located right next door, Maker's Adda is our dedicated community innovation space. Move beyond textbooks and start building. Access a fully equipped makerspace, attend workshops, and find mentorship in:

  • Robotics & Al
  • Drone Making
  • Mathematics & Engineering

Science is for Everyone From young explorers to science enthusiasts and curious adults, ParSEC is a playground for the mind.

Visitor Guidelines

  • Booking & Tickets: Advance booking via BookMyShow recommended. Tickets are non-refundable and non- exchangeable. Valid only for the booked slot; no early/late entry. Max 10 tickets per booking.
  • Entry Rules: Kids below 3 enter free (ID proof required). Kids under 12 must be with an adult. Smoking, alcohol, and drugs are prohibited; entry denied to those under the influence.
  • Venue Guidelines: Arrive 10 minutes early. No food or beverages allowed. Lost & found services unavailable.
  • Conduct & Liability: Unlawful resale leads to ticket cancellation. Disruptive visitors may be removed without a refund. Visitors bear costs for any damage.
  • Fees & Policies: An internet handling fee may apply. Check the final amount before payment. Admission rights reserved.
